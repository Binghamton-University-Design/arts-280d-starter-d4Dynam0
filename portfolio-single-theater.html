Theater Symptoms

The title of this book instantly caught my eye; providing so many possiblities creatively. This happened to be my biggest challenge, as I could not settle on a design I was satisfied with for weeks. I left my comfort zone and utilized only a simple image and contrasting color to communicate the paradoxical nature of a life on stage; and the interal discord that inevitably comes to define the life of an artist.
