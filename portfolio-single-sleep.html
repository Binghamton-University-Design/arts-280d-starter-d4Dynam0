Waking Up

Beginning this project, the central concept (sleep); and my intutive graphic form gave me a strong foundation to work from. I was challenged in terms of readibiliy, color, and hierarchy. There was frequent struggle to not oversaturate with text and color. Utliizing reductive design, I learned the value of keeping only what was nessesary and maintaining balance between the text and visual narrative.
