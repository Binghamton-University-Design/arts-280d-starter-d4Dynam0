How do the engines start?

For my first infographic project, the goal was to interpret and design a new layout from an old design book from the 1950’s. The information was laid out accross four pages, of which I was assigned to put into a cohesive whole. This was a challenge for my layout and composition skills; and at this point I began to heavily emphasize sketching in my design process.
