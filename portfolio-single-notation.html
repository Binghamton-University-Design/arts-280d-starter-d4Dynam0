Writing in Motion

This book is a brief look at Labanotiation, a dance notation system devoped by Rudolph Laban, for the purpose of communicating movement in a written form. As a former ballet dancer, It was surprising to have never heard of the system before. It is not widely used or taught; but it was an exciting and personally significant topic to layout for my first ever book project. These are few excerpts from the full book.
